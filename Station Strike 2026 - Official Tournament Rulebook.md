# Station Strike 2026 – Official Tournament Rulebook

## Event overview
Station Strike marks Station 35’s ambition to deliver a new French Counter-Strike event. The tournament is built around clear competition rules, a strong offline framework, and an experience designed for both players and spectators.

## Dates and venue
- Event dates: July 22 to July 26, 2026
- Venue: Station 35
- Full address: 31 rue Xavier Grall, 35700 Rennes, France

## Competition schedule
- July 22: Group A & B
- July 23: Group C & D
- July 24: Round of 16
- July 25: Quarterfinals and Semifinals
- July 26: Third-place match and Grand Final

## Tournament format
- 24 teams in total
- 4 groups of 4 teams, each group played in GSL bracket format
- Top 8 teams by VRS seeding are directly qualified for the Round of 16
- Top 2 teams from each group qualify for the Round of 16

## Prize pool distribution
- 1st place: €7,500
- 2nd place: €3,750
- 3rd place: €2,250
- 4th place: €1,500

## Registration process
- Registration opens: June 23, 18:00 GMT
- Registration closes: July 12, 22:00 GMT

Registration steps:
1. Complete the registration form.
2. Payment terms are sent to the registration email.
3. Teams have 24 hours to complete payment and send proof of payment after receiving the payment terms.
4. If payment is not completed in time, the slot is passed to the next team in line.
5. Wait for confirmation from the organizer.

Teams are accepted in order of payment proof reception. No invites are issued.
