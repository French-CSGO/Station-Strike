# Valve Specific Information

For seeding purposes, Station Strike 2026 will use the official Global VRS ranking update published in July.

## Tournament details
- Event: Station Strike 2026
- Tournament dates: 22 July 2026 to 26 July 2026
- Tournament location: Station 35, 31 rue Xavier Grall, 35700 Rennes, France
- Tournament format: LAN event
- Total teams: 24
- Open registration: yes
- Direct invites: no
- Seeding policy: 8 best teams by VRS are directly qualified to Round of 16, remaining 16 teams play 4 GSL groups (A/B/C/D), top 2 of each group qualify to Round of 16

## Registration timeline
- Registration opens: June 23, 18:00 GMT
- Registration closes: July 12, 22:00 GMT
- Team slots are confirmed by payment proof reception order

## Play schedule
- July 22: Group A & B
- July 23: Group C & D
- July 24: Round of 16
- July 25: Quarterfinals & Semifinals
- July 26: Third-place match & Grand Final
